I am a second year Ph.D. student in the Operations Research & Information Engineering Department at Cornell University. I am co-advised by Prof. Jim Dai and Prof. Manxi Wu. My research focuses on reinforcement learning and game theories in network systems. I have rich experience in applying the deep neural network to solve complicated stochastic systems. Before I came to Cornell, I completed my master's degree in Statistics at Columbia University. I obtained my undergraduate degree from the University of Washington with a double major in Computer Science and Statistics.
